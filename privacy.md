Privacy Policy

Last updated: [15/07/2026]

This privacy policy describes how Cyber-tester
collects, uses, and protects information when you use it to sign in with 
TikTok via TikTok's official OAuth login system.

1. Information We Collect
This App only requests the minimum permissions needed for testing purposes, 
currently limited to basic public profile information (such as username 
and user ID) made available through TikTok's official API.

2. How We Use Information
Any information obtained is used solely for local testing and development 
purposes. It is not sold, shared, or used for advertising, marketing, or 
any commercial purpose.

3. Data Storage
This App does not store TikTok account data on any server. Access tokens 
are used locally on the developer's own device for the duration of testing 
and are not transmitted to or stored by any third party.

4. Data Sharing
We do not share any information obtained through this App with third 
parties.

5. Data Retention & Deletion
No persistent data is retained beyond the local testing session. Users may 
revoke access to their TikTok account at any time via TikTok's account 
settings or by requesting token revocation directly.
